Understanding your warranty:

You have two options when choosing your warranty.

  1. Rodent Exclusion Warranty with Simpson Pest Control Services.
  2. Rodent Exclusion Warranty without Simpson Pest Control Services.

The coverage for the two are the same. However, the cost is exclusive to the services you have with us that are pre existing or that you purchase along with your exclusion program. Qualifying programs are recurring pest, termite, mosquito, or monthly rodent monitoring. Any of these programs count as an ongoing service.

Our Renewable warranty when not bundled with any of our services will cost you $269.00 yearly (Price can vary depending on the square footage of the home).

If not bundled with one of our ongoing services your renewal will cost you $289.00 yearly (Price can vary depending on square footage of the home).

Your annual payment is due 360 days after initial service each year and will continue to be renewed every 360 days or until you request that it be canceled. Your warranty is also transferable. If you should choose to sell the home or the property should change hands the new resident can have the warranty transferred into their name and the coverage will continue as before. There is a $150 transfer fee that is required along with a visual inspection to make sure the home is still properly excluded according to the agreement on file.

This certificate is proof of your coverage, if at any time you wish to cancel the warranty, you must notify Simpson Pest Control 30 days prior to your intent to cancel. Canceling by email is acceptable.

What does your warranty cover:

  •                Sealing any new entry points created by rodents or wildlife.
  •                Resealing any entry points previously sealed that have been damaged by rodents or wildlife.
  •                Re-sanitizing of the attic if it should be deemed necessary.

What your warranty does not cover:

  •                Trapping and relocation of larger wildlife such as skunks, raccoons, or opossum.
  •                One-way trap installation for squirrels and larger wildlife.
