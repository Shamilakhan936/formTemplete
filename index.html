Nature and Scope of Agreement: Bed Bug Inspection and Treatment Plan.

The agreement contained within has been designed for the management of bed bugs. However, due to the biology and behavior of this difficult pest, there is no guarantee that bed bugs will be eliminated or prevented from continuing to infest the treated areas.

This treatment is for the current bed bug infestation only. Bed bugs are an introduced pest and the re-occurrence of bed bugs within the facility can occur at any given time. Re-introduction of bed bugs is not covered in this agreement. All recommendations must be followed by customer to guarantee elimination.

Step 5 Control

During each of the follow-up visits an evaluation will be made to determine if control has been achieved. The company will declare CONTROL when no more bed bugs are observed during the follow-up visit AND the client has not reported any visible bed bug activity or experienced any bites during the immediate preceding 30 day period. If bites are occurring or bed bugs are found to be present then follow-up visits will continue until CONTROL can be declared or client wishes to end the agreement.

Step 6 Elimination

The company will contact the client approximately 45 days after CONTROL has been declared to determine if ELIMINATION has been achieved. The infestation will be declared "eliminated" when no more bed bugs are observed by the client AND no bites are reported by the client for a period of 30 consecutive days after CONTROL has been declared. There is no charge for this communication. The client understands that if we are unable to make contact then the ELIIMINATION provision is void and the program reverts back to Step 4. (When combining the control and elimination step, 60 consecutive days will have passed with no bugs or bites have being reported or found). It is understood that in some situations, the infestation may never be declared "eliminated". In these cases ongoing services will be required to manage the bed bug infestation and no service plan will be granted. If during the elimination and Service Plan period additional furniture (new or used) is brought into the structure the elimination period may be void.

Step 7 Service Plan

The client will receive a 90 service plan which begins on the day that ELIMINATION has been declared. During the 90 days, if bed bugs are found to be present, the company will service the property at no additional charge.
